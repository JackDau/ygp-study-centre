## Foundations of YourGP

With a clear vision to meaningfully improve health outcomes and guided by the timeless principles of care and compassion, Doctors John and Mel Deery set out to provide quality healthcare through their first general practice in 2012.

### Our Why

**Love others as you love yourself**: We want to deliver the kind of care we'd want for our own families and loved ones. It just happens that we are in healthcare.

### Our Vision

To meaningfully improve health outcomes by offering healthcare that is caring, innovative, and consistently excellent.

### Our Values

#### 1. Care
Genuine care for our patients, team members, and communities is at the heart of what we do.

#### 2. Excellence
We give our very best every day, knowing there's always more to learn and improve.

#### 3. Kaizen (Continuous Improvement)
We believe failure is not a loss. It's a win. We learn and adapt. We grow step by step, learning from every challenge, and always seeking better ways to serve.

## The YourGP Flywheel

A continuous cycle of improvement, where each action builds on the last, creating a powerful force that drives us towards our goal of exceptional healthcare.

```
        Retain and Attract
        the Right Clinicians
                |
                |
    Invest in the Best
    Facilities and People
                |
                |
    Create a Culture of Caring,
    Excellence, and Innovation
                |
                |
        Grow Resources
                |
                |
    Retain and Attract
    the Right Patients
```

### 1. Retain and Attract the Right Clinicians

Skilled and compassionate clinicians form the backbone of YourGP. We're committed to finding and supporting Doctors and Nurses who share our values.

### 2. Create a Culture of Caring, Excellence, and Innovation

A healthy culture doesn't happen by accident. We work hard to foster an environment where care, learning, and progress are second nature.

### 3. Retain and Attract the Right Doctors AND the Right Patients

Years of caring have taught us that the best Doctor-Patient relationships are not by chance. They are the result of carefully choosing not only the right doctors but also the right patients.

### 4. Grow Resources

By carefully managing our finances and operations, we ensure we have the resources needed to support our team and community.

### 5. Invest in the Best Facilities and People

We are intentional about creating spaces where our team and patients feel supported, while also investing in training and development to help everyone thrive.

## Unique Practices That Reflect Our Ethos

While some of our management practices are inspired by thought leaders, others are deeply rooted in the ethos of YourGP's owners and leadership team. These practices reflect the personal, people-first culture of our organisation.

### 1. Celebrating Our People

- Our online "Brag Book" is a space where team members share thanks and recognition for each other's contributions
- We've created a "Get to Know Everyone" Wall featuring fun facts and photos of our team members at every practice
- On birthdays, we deliver each team member's favourite cake to celebrate them in a meaningful way
- Once a month, our coaches come together to learn, connect, and plan for the future -- all while enjoying time together as a team

### 2. Creating a Personal, Non-Corporate Culture

As a privately owned business where the owners are practising doctors and work within the organisation, over the years, a warm and personal culture has evolved.

Despite the demanding and fast-paced nature of healthcare, our team members tend to stay with us for many years, a testament to the supportive environment we've built.

### 3. A Listening and Feedback-Rich Culture

Inspired by the book *Thank You for the Feedback*, we make giving and receiving feedback a part of our day-to-day interactions. This fosters openness, trust, and continuous improvement, aligning with our values of Care, Excellence and Kaizen.

### 4. Hiring for Cultural and Values Fit

Inspired by *Foolproof Hiring*, we dedicate significant resources to finding the right cultural and values fit.

Recruiting right is a cornerstone of our philosophy, ensuring that every team member aligns with our values. We know that people aligned with our values come with batteries included -- they are driven and have a sense of purpose, allowing YourGP to become the stage on which they excel.

### 5. Structured 1-on-1 Coaching

We don't manage -- we coach. Managers, or "coaches," invest in regular, structured 1-on-1 meetings with their teams. This practice reinforces our commitment to care.

### 6. Goal Setting

Inspired by John Doerr's OKR (Objectives & Key Results) framework, we set goals that are aligned, transparent, and accountable. This system ensures everyone at YourGP is working toward a shared vision, reflecting our value of Excellence.

### 7. Lean Thinking

Lean thinking, a concept borrowed from manufacturing, is central to our way of working. Inspired by the PDSA (Plan-Do-Study-Act) framework, we encourage experiments, celebrate failures, and learn from them. This way, we can improve our processes and services, reflecting our dedication to Kaizen and continuous improvement.

### 8. Investing in Learning from the Best

Our management philosophy has been shaped by the principles in books like:
- *Scaling Up* (Rockefeller Habits)
- *The E-Myth*
- *Profit First*
- *Good to Great*
- *Excellence Wins*
- *Bringing Out the Best in People*
- *Radical Candour*
- *The Heart of Business*
- *Start with Why*
- *Hiring Staff* (Topgrading)
- *How to Win*
- *What a Unicorn Knows*
- *5 Dysfunctions of a Team*
- And many others!

These frameworks guide us in creating a highly engaged organisation (which we measure using the world-renowned Gallup framework)

## How These Practices Strengthen Our Culture

These unique practices, whether inspired by external frameworks or born from our own ethos, create a workplace where team members feel valued, celebrated, and empowered. Together, they reinforce our core values of Care, Excellence, and Kaizen, where our people are dedicated to delivering exceptional care.

**Our Journey has just begun.** We know we're not perfect -- but we are committed. Committed to learning, growing, and improving.
