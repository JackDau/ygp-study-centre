## Why We Created the Values and Outcome Matrix

We believe that everyone should be recognised not just for their job title, but for how they contribute to the YourGP community (Patients, Doctors and Staff).

In many workplaces, two people with the same role--like receptionist--are rewarded the same, even if one is just meeting expectations and another is leading, supporting others, and driving improvements.

**That's why we created the Values and Outcome Matrix** -- it is totally disconnected from job titles.

---

## What Does This Mean?

- You can stay in the role you love and still grow, thrive, and be rewarded for your contributions
- You don't have to become a manager to "level up" -- you can lead from wherever you are
- This matrix helps clarify what growth looks like and makes it easier to see what the "next level" means

### Example

- A receptionist might be doing exactly what's expected of their role and meeting all the behaviours of a "Sailor" -- steady, reliable, and focused
- Another receptionist might be taking initiative, mentoring others, and solving problems like a "Skipper"
- Both contribute meaningfully, but their outcomes are different -- and they should be recognised accordingly

---

## Why This Matters

We want to make it clear that you don't need to "outgrow" your role to keep progressing. You can stay in your role, build on your strengths, and still grow.

This approach also helps us have open conversations about recognition, growth, and even salary -- so everyone understands how contributions are valued and rewarded.

**We're committed to building a place where everyone can grow and be recognised for their impact -- no matter what their job title says.**

## Value: Care

### Sailor Level
- The YourGP community is welcomed and acknowledged kindly
- The YourGP community leave interactions feeling heard and valued
- Patients and GPs experience timely and empathetic support
- Keeps the YourGP community -- patients, colleagues, and clinicians -- in mind during daily tasks and decisions

### Mid-Shipman Level (Sailor plus...)
- Teams enjoy strong, caring relationships
- A culture of excellence is evident in daily operations
- Communication ensures that team members feel valued and understood
- Helps the team focus on actions that improve the YourGP community's experience

### Skipper Level (Mid-Shipman plus...)
- Systems are in place to ensure consistent and reliable care experiences across the organisation
- Systems make it easy for teams to put the YourGP community first in their work
- Team members understand how their work impacts the BHAG
- Authentic relationships within the YourGP community build trust and foster confidence in the BHAG
- Systems to support teams with structured feedback mechanisms are in place
- Team and individual achievements are celebrated

### Commander Level (Skipper plus...)
- Every major initiative supports and strengthens the experience of the YourGP community
- YourGP community report high satisfaction with team
- Team culture reflects care, excellence, and kaizen
- Teams are Cohesive (Lencioni)
- Systems in place to ensure consistent team performance and operational reliability
- Mentors are supported to prepare them for potential leadership roles
- Teams are inspired to achieve BHAG

---

## Value: Excellence

### Sailor Level
- Processes are followed consistently
- Work outputs meet or exceed defined quality standards

### Mid-Shipman Level (Sailor plus...)
- Operational processes are improved regularly
- Clarity of communication improves processes and reduces misunderstandings
- Compliance/regulatory requirements are understood
- Meaningful change is supported and the 'why' is clearly explained
- Teams experiment with innovative approaches and adapt based on outcomes

### Skipper Level (Mid-Shipman plus...)
- Team roles are clearly defined
- Team members' contributions to operational health is clear and measured
- Financial responsibilities are clear and achieved
- Teams are high performing, aligned with Organisational goals
- Organisational tools (e.g. Playbook) evolve to support growing needs and services
- Organisational Teams are coached to deliver on performance expectations
- Quality & Compliance frameworks are installed
- Strategies to ensure consistent application of the organisation's values are shared
- Skippers actively support other Skippers by providing feedback and assisting in addressing challenges collaboratively

### Commander Level (Skipper plus...)
- Evidence of excellence in service quality and operational efficiency
- Core values are installed in Hiring, training, and management practices
- Commanders hold each other accountable for the organization's values and strategic goals
- Adapting to feedback and driving innovation is DNA
- Improvement initiatives are championed, making choices on what is important using feedback to refine and adapt when needed

---

## Value: Kaizen (Continuous Improvement)

### Sailor Level
- Small, incremental improvements are regularly suggested and trialled
- Feedback is given and received from peers and supervisors

### Mid-Shipman Level (Sailor plus...)
- Sailors participate or lead defined improvement cycles (e.g. PDSA)
- Feedback systems are integrated into all activities
- Collaboration ensures innovative ideas are trialled effectively
- Change impact on the YourGP community is considered before action
- Vulnerability is modelled through openness to feedback and learning from mistakes
- Learning is ongoing, with best practices and learnings shared to improve the organisation and the way things are done
